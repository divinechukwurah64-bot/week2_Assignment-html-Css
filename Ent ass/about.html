About DigiSkills Academy

We're on a mission to close the digital skills gap in Africa — one learner at a time.

Our Story

Built for African Learners

DigiSkills Academy was founded in 2020 by a group of tech professionals who saw a massive gap between the demand for digital talent and the availability of quality, affordable training in Nigeria and across Africa.

We started with just 3 courses and 50 students. Today, we have over 5,000 alumni who are working as developers, designers, data analysts, and digital marketers across the globe.

Our curriculum is built around real-world projects, not just theory. Every student leaves with a portfolio they can show to employers and clients.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and industry-relevant digital skills training to every Nigerian and African youth — regardless of their background, location, or prior experience.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading digital skills academy, producing a new generation of tech-enabled professionals who can compete and thrive in the global digital economy.

Our Values

Excellence, inclusion, innovation, and community. We believe that when one person learns, the whole community benefits. We celebrate every win — big or small.

Our Promise

Every student who completes a course at DigiSkills Academy will have job-ready skills, a completed portfolio project, and access to our alumni network and career support team.
